Welcome to the official page of our upcoming game!

From the amazing minds of Kaylin Arias and Emily Chen, we present Company of Tomorrow.

This is a cool live action game where you play as Allen, the brand new leader of a company called Veritas.

We at Funticing want to drain your negativity not your wallet.

Reputation Meter

The game will have something called a reputation meter.

This is because, the main character Allen will be in charge of the company called Nova.

Since being in charge of a company means deciding when to delay or release something, reputation is also massively important.

This entails choosing the exact words to say to journalists, or public statements.

Too low reputation and it leads to a Game Over.

Ability to go anywhere the player wants

Unlike most live action games, where the character is already in a certain room, our game will have options to go to certain places./p>

But be careful! If you go into places other than the company's HQ too many times, you could get fired.

This effect is only at certain times however, so do not be too afraid.

Non-linear Gameplay

The player will be able to choose how to do pretty much anything in the company

However, there will be consequences for every action you take.

Characters

Allen

The main character of the game, Allen has just been promoted at his company, Veritas.

However, he wants make the company have record high profits, which is hard because Veritas isn't doing so well now.

Your job is to make the best company possible, dodging reporters accusations, investor complaints and more!

Stark Schatte

One of the antagonists of the game, he is a traditionalist, hell bent on making the company his way.

Stark does not realize that his meddling is partly the reason why the company is failing.

Roseandra Johnson

Roseandra has been in the company for a while, and is known for being eccentric.

She is also known for being a compulsive liar, and almost everyone does not trust her.

Roseandra had a tough upbringing, which may explain this.

Vera Fairweather

A new intern, who wants to have her ideas heard.

But since she is new in the company, no one listens.

Maybe her ideas are actually good??
